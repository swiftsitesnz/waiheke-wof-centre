The team at Waiheke WoF Centre

Our Story

When you live on an island, you need a mechanic you can rely on. Someone who gives you an honest assessment, does quality work, and doesn't charge the earth for it. That's what we've been doing since 2004.

We handle everything from WoF inspections to full repairs, diagnostics, tyres, and towing through our AA and partner network. The workshop is equipped with modern tools, but we haven't lost the personal touch — you'll always deal with a real person who'll explain things clearly.

Most of our customers come through word of mouth, and many have been with us for years. We're not the flashiest shop around, but we are the one that gets it right.
